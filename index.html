From first build to the latest signal.

  1. v1.0.0 2026-06-13

    Initial early-access launch

    • Store, party, matches, wiki, and social surfaces in one companion
    • Riot RSO sign-in handoff
    • Home, store, party, matches, loadout, and social readouts
  2. v1.0.2 2026-06-23

    VALSYNC rebrand and privacy hardening

    • Rebrand from ValPaw to VALSYNC; package moved to com.valsync.app
    • OAuth PKCE flow with encrypted token storage
    • Redesigned match details and replay with tactical-telemetry HUD
    • Full i18n for English, Arabic, Turkish, and German
    • R8 minification and debug-only cleartext traffic
  3. v1.0.3 2026-06-27

    Riot sign-in cleanup

    • Full-screen Riot RSO page, edge-to-edge
    • System back handler and floating close button for cancellation
    • Strip WebView UA marker so "Sign in with Google" works
  4. v1.0.4 2026-07-04

    Watchlist alerts and lineup details

    • Skin watchlist alerts for store and Night Market
    • Match trends and advisor, plus lineup details
    • Riot auth WebView moved to a standalone Activity (fixes focus and keystroke bugs)
  5. v1.0.5 2026-07-05

    Clutch and multi-kill readouts

    • Per-player clutch %, plus double, triple, quadra, and ACE counts
    • First-blood detection by real game-time ordering; rounded HS%
    • Tap a player row to expand extended stats (ACS, KDA, K-D, HS chips)
    • Language switch cold-restart fix via AlarmManager and PendingIntent
  6. v1.0.6 2026-07-06 Latest

    Live match panel in Party

    • Party hosts a live match panel with dodge dialog, enemy intel, and telemetry probe
    • Lineups rebuilt with loading states; mock fallback data removed
    • Login: VALSYNC logo asset replaces the diamond brand mark
    • Riot API client parallelizes live-match player builds (async/awaitAll)
    • Settings: real BuildConfig.VERSION_NAME instead of hardcoded value

Built for players who want signal, not spectacle.

Do I need a Riot account?

VALSYNC is designed around Riot sign-in handoff, so account-specific surfaces stay tied to the player who owns them.

Where do ads show up?

They do not. The product promise is a companion surface without banner slots, engagement traps, or monetized tracking.

What can I check from the app?

The preview covers the main VALSYNC loop: Home, Store, Party, Matches, Loadout, and Social.

How does the store check work?

VALSYNC surfaces the shop, Night Market, and a skin watchlist so you can glance at what is available without opening multiple browser tabs.

Can I track my squad or party?

Yes. The party module shows who is online, who is mid-match, and what roles the stack is missing before the lobby fills up.

Does VALSYNC collect or sell my data?

No. The product promise is a companion without banner ads, tracking pixels, or data brokerage. Telemetry surfaces explain product state, never user surveillance.
